About Invoice Processor

This tool processes invoice PDFs by:

  • Extracting metadata (date, customer, order number) from invoices
  • Renaming them to a standard format: dd.mm_Customer_Order_Code.pdf
  • Cross-checking tax invoices against Finance invoice
  • Generating a CSV report with comparison results

Privacy

All processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No files are uploaded to any server. Your data never leaves your machine.

Technologies

Built with PDF.js (text extraction) and JSZip (archive creation). Pure client-side JavaScript, no backend required.
